The railgun is the latest development in the field of weapons in the C sector in the Category B laboratory. When fired, an electromagnetic pulse may occur that knocks out electronics in a small radius. The weapon hangar has access to the surface on a cargo platform. The firing range is 3 kilometers. The main disadvantage is that the rail cooling time is too long.
